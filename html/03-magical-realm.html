Still. There was something unsettling about the idea that Oriko would be expecting her in advance. It was rather like Homura calling her predictable, except even more so. It was almost a question of free will.

I'm distracted, she realized, and I should be keeping an eye out,/ /in case of any surprises. Was that just the breeze, or was there a rustling sound in the hedge -
